Dataset Summary

  • The dataset is a list of companies in the S&P 500 (Standard and Poor's 500). The S&P 500 is a capitalization-weighted index of the top 500 publicly listed stocks in the US (top 500 by market capitalization). The dataset includes a list of all the stocks contained therein and associated key financials such as price, market capitalization, earnings, etc.
    The dataset is free and can be obtained from the Standard and Poor's official website (registration is a pre-requisite).
  • Type of data in the dataset: Numerical (market cap), Categorical (Sector, Name).
  • The objective here is to see if by applying some of the concepts we learned in the class we can somehow represent how important is a sector. For example, is Technology more important than Finance in terms of Market Capitalization?

Sketch Summary
