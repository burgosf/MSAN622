Project Dataset

Original location of the dataset..

The dataset is free and can be obtained from the Standard and Poor's official website (registration is a pre-requisite).

Type of data in the dataset:

  • Numerical: Market Capitalization.
  • Categorical: Sector, Name.

    • I choose this dataset because I used to work in finance for several years and never encountered interesting and eye catchy vizualizations. The objective here is to see if by applying some of the concepts we learned in the class we can somehow represent how important is a sector. For example, is Technology more importance than Finance in terms of Market Capitalization?
